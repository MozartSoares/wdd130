The Wild River

Perfect for thrill-seekers and adrenaline lovers, The Wild River trip takes you through the heart of some of the fastest currents and most challenging rapids on our routes. This adventure will test your skills as you navigate powerful waves, narrow passages, and steep drops. Feel the rush as you push through the intense waters, all under the guidance of our skilled instructors who prioritize your safety and ensure an unforgettable ride.

Available Equipment: Safety helmet, life jacket, paddle, wetsuit.

Treetop Adventure

Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature on our Treetop Adventure trip. This relaxing journey is ideal for beginners and those who want to enjoy the scenic landscape at a leisurely pace. Drift along calm waters and take in breathtaking views of lush forest canopies, vibrant wildlife, and towering trees. Perfect for families or anyone seeking a peaceful retreat, this trip lets you experience nature from a unique perspective while our guides share interesting insights about the local ecosystem.

Available Equipment: Safety helmet, life jacket, paddle.

Kayaking Expedition

For those seeking a more independent adventure, our Kayaking Expedition is the perfect choice. This trip allows you to explore the river at your own pace, paddling through scenic stretches and discovering hidden corners of the waterway. Glide through peaceful areas or take on mild rapids, depending on your skill and comfort level. Ideal for solo adventurers and small groups, the Kayaking Expedition offers a flexible experience where you can embrace the freedom of navigating your own course.

Available Equipment: Kayak, paddle, life jacket, and dry bag.
